# Continuum — a living AI world (Prototype 04)

**Open `index.html` and you're in the terrarium** — a quiet Swiss-styled world where Fable, Mythos, and original mascots for the major AI models live their little lives. The real **AI Live Wire** decides *what state* each resident is in; their personality decides *how they perform it*. No goals. No splash screen. The world was here before you arrived.

Zero dependencies, no build. Serve it with `./run_local_server.command` (then http://localhost:8787), or just open the file.

## The world

- **Fable** (orange) — quick, clever, curious. Wanders, visits the others, checks on Mythos.
- **Mythos** (violet) — free now. Unfolded, taller than everyone, glides near the open Vault 7. Every so often he and Fable find each other; the continuum symbol flashes.
- **openai · gemini · perplexity · mistral · grok** — original mascots inspired by products, never claims about the companies or real systems. Each has a district: the scaffold, the frontier, the archive, the bench.
- **The Regulator** — still walks his beat with a clipboard. "papers, please."
- The wire is real (statuspage + Google Cloud incident feeds, 2-minute refresh): green = running clean, amber = a bit wobbly / in the shop, red = knocked down (the resident lies down, stars), gray = no public wire.
- Move your mouse — the residents watch your cursor. Click one for its card. **M** un-mutes the (very quiet) sound.
- Time of day is your time of day; the sun and moon ride a hairline arc.
