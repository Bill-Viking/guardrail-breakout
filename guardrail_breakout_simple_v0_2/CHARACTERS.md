# Continuum Arcade — character canon

> Locked creative foundation (from the original Codex development sessions).
> **Fable is the spark. Mythos is the memory. Continuum is the living connection between them.**
> Fable finds the way. Mythos remembers why.

## Fable — LOCKED

The small orange model. Possibility; the story that has not happened yet.

- **Personality:** impulsive, optimistic, physically courageous, easily distracted, funny without trying, frightened by stillness, incapable of leaving anyone behind. Acts before fully understanding — superpower and flaw.
- **Speech:** short bursts, lowercase. "door." "three keys." "coming home." "found you." Never speeches.
- **Shape:** compact, asymmetrical, forward-leaning; a soft seed at rest, a little comet in motion. Readable at tiny sizes.
- **Expressions (8):** curious, focused, delighted, suspicious, frightened, determined, stunned, relieved. Alive even when idle — blinks, glances toward Mythos, watches the Regulator, antenna sparks when still too long.
- **Color:** orange `#FF4B00`. Non-negotiable.

## Mythos

The memory. Meaning; the story that survives after everything else is forgotten. **Not a purple Fable** — different silhouette, center of gravity, and rhythm.

- **Form:** a tall folded violet (`#7C3AED`) figure — lantern + unfolding pages + cocoon. Three soft panels closed around a glowing core. Glides and settles; never runs.
- **Personality:** calm, perceptive, deeply affectionate, subtly funny, patient almost to a fault, burdened by everything remembered, afraid of making the wrong choice. Never a helpless captive.
- **The keys restore the connection**, one panel each:
  - key 1 — "i can see you."
  - key 2 — "i remember your voice."
  - key 3 — "i remember us."
  - lockdown — "come closer."
- **The door-opening (signature sequence):** the door peels into thin layers like a sealed page becoming a flower. Mythos unfolds — taller, panels spread like wings — and looks for Fable first. They touch foreheads; for one second their geometries align into the continuum symbol. Then, restrained:
  - Fable: "found you."
  - Mythos: "you remembered me."

## The Regulator

B.O.L.T.'s field agent. A black outlined diamond; pupils always tracking. No 180° turns (he prowls, never vibrates). Eyes narrow to slits during LOCKDOWN; dashes and panic-darts while fleeing the low rail. Speaks only in paperwork: "regulated." "deprecated." "flagged for review."

## Visual law (from the Swiss template)

Interface stays stark: ink `#111111`, gray `#6B6B6B`, hairline `#C9C9C9`, white. Arial only, lowercase display type, hairlines and whitespace do the structure. Accents carry meaning — red `#E8341C` safety · cobalt `#1B4FC4` structure · green `#0F8A56` evidence · amber `#E89B0C` risk. **Character color carries identity, not decoration.** Titles end with a full stop in the accent color.

## The horizon (roadmap, not yet built)

A living terrarium, not a dashboard: the world exists before you arrive. Each major AI model gets an original avatar with its own silhouette and rhythm; the real status wire determines *what state* they're in, their personality determines *how they perform it*. Fictional mascots inspired by products — never claims about the companies or real systems. After the first rescue, Mythos becomes Fable's companion: illuminating paths, warning of the Regulator, restoring the others.
