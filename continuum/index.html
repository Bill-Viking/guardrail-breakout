the story of how it started: field 01 — the vault run ↗  ·  what is this? ↗

continuum is a terrarium of the real ai world. every resident is a fictional mascot for a real model — their moods follow the live status wires below, and headlines from hacker news become their news days. nothing here claims to be the companies or the systems themselves; the facts are real, the performance is theirs. when a local model (ollama) is running, it quietly directs the day like a showrunner — turning today's headlines into each mascot's story and remembering the plot across days; without it, the world runs on its own templates.

the legend: fable (orange) is the spark — anthropic's wire moves him. mythos (violet) is the memory — fable freed him from vault 7 once (that's field 01), and now fable visits him every day: that's the "ritual" poster. openai (cobalt) builds the tower — one block per shipping mood; it never resets. gemini (green) explores — the little green flags are her trail, every place she's been. perplexity (amber) files the archive. mistral (brick) tinkers at the bench. grok (ink) is nocturnal and chases moths. the regulator patrols, clipboard out.

you: hover anyone to see what they're doing. click a resident for their card — its buttons (explore · build · rest · visit fable · work) tell them how to spend the next ten minutes. drag a resident anywhere and drop them; they'll react, and the director takes note. click empty ground to leave a spark — someone will come look. click a chip in the live feed for details and news. click the ticker line to open the story. m for sound. the world saves itself and keeps aging while you're gone.
